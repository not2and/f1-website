Current Teams

Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team

Founded in 2010, Mercedes has been a dominant force in F1, winning multiple Constructors' Championships.

Scuderia Ferrari

The oldest and most successful team in F1 history, Ferrari has a rich legacy and passionate fanbase.

Red Bull Racing

Known for their innovative designs and strong performance, Red Bull has secured several championships in recent years.

McLaren F1 Team

With a history dating back to 1963, McLaren is one of the most successful teams in the sport.

Alpine F1 Team

Formerly known as Renault, Alpine aims to return to the top of the grid with a strong lineup and competitive car.

Aston Martin Cognizant Formula One Team

Rebranded from Racing Point, Aston Martin is looking to build on its recent successes and challenge for podiums.

Scuderia AlphaTauri

The sister team to Red Bull, AlphaTauri focuses on developing young talent and innovative designs.

Haas F1 Team

An American team that has made significant strides since its debut in 2016, Haas aims to be a consistent points scorer.

Williams Racing

One of the most historic teams in F1, Williams is working hard to return to its former glory.

Alfa Romeo Racing Orlen

With a strong heritage, Alfa Romeo is focused on improving its performance and competitiveness in the midfield.
